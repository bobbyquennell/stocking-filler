How to Apply a Face Mask

Learn how with Vanda Serrador

Our Facialist and Skincare Expert Vanda Serrador shows us the best way to apply a facial mask in this beauty video tutorial. She recommends using a brush or your fingertips to apply the mask to targeted areas of the face. She suggests using a face mask once or twice a week, or more frequently if you have oily to combination skin. For best results, you should also cleanse your face before applying a face mask!
